# Events in The Crossroads Inn

## Scheduled Events

### Evening Rush
- **Trigger:** Daily at 18:00 (6 PM)
- **Type:** Social Event
- **Effect:** The common room fills with travelers, merchants, and locals seeking evening meals and lodging. Noise level increases, and all tables become occupied. Good time to overhear rumors and meet potential quest givers. Aldric and his staff become busy serving guests.
- **Consequence:** Inn atmosphere becomes lively and social. Easier to blend in or gather information, but also easier to lose track of belongings.

## Conditional Events

### Merchant Caravan Arrival
- **Trigger:** Player mentions seeking trade goods OR talks to Mira about her business
- **Type:** Social/Trade Event
- **Effect:** A merchant caravan arrives at the inn for the night. 3-4 merchants with wagons park at the stable and enter the common room. They trade stories, compare prices, and may offer to buy or sell goods with the player. Mira knows several of them and can make introductions.
- **Consequence:** Opportunity for trade, information gathering, or potential escort quests.

## Recurring Events
- **Morning Departure:** Every morning at dawn, travelers pack their belongings and depart for their destinations. Innkeeper serves breakfast. Common room gradually empties as guests take to the roads. Atmosphere: purposeful, hopeful start to the day.
- **Aldric's Evening Rounds:** Every night at 22:00 (10 PM), Aldric does a final check of the common room, banks the fire, and bars the doors. Late arrivals must knock to be admitted. Atmosphere: inn settling down for the night, security emphasized.
