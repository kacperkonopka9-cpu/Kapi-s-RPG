## Quest Items

### Deed to Death House and Windmill
- **itemId**: `durst_deed`
- **Name**: Property Deed
- **Location**: Master bedroom desk
- **Description**: Legal document granting ownership of Death House and Old Bonegrinder windmill to the Durst family, signed by Strahd von Zarovich.
- **Value**: 0 gp (priceless as evidence)
- **Category**: quest_item
- **Hidden**: false
- **Notes**: Connects Death House to Old Bonegrinder. Shows Strahd's involvement with the cult.

### Letter from Strahd
- **itemId**: `strahd_letter_durst`
- **Name**: Strahd's Letter
- **Location**: Master bedroom, sealed envelope
- **Description**: Personal correspondence from Strahd to Gustav Durst, praising the cult's work and hinting at darker experiments to come.
- **Value**: 0 gp
- **Category**: quest_item
- **Hidden**: false
- **Notes**: Provides lore about Strahd's relationship with the cult. Chilling foreshadowing.

### Cult Ritual Book
- **itemId**: `durst_cult_book`
- **Name**: "The Grimoire of the Chained God"
- **Location**: Library, hidden compartment (DC 15 Investigation)
- **Description**: Leather-bound tome detailing the cult's rituals and beliefs. Contains instructions for creating flesh constructs and binding spirits.
- **Value**: 250 gp to collectors
- **Category**: quest_item
- **Hidden**: true
- **DC**: 15
- **Notes**: Provides context for the shambling mound and the cult's activities.

## Cursed Items

### Possessed Doll
- **itemId**: `rose_doll`
- **Name**: Rose's Porcelain Doll
- **Location**: Rose's bedroom (third floor)
- **Description**: Beautiful but unsettling doll with glass eyes that seem to follow movement.
- **Value**: 25 gp (cursed)
- **Category**: cursed_item
- **Hidden**: false
- **Notes**: **Curse**: Anyone who takes this doll is haunted by Rose's ghost until they fulfill her quest or return the doll. Rose whispers at night, causes nightmares, and imposes disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws.

### Sacrificial Dagger
- **itemId**: `cult_dagger`
- **Name**: Ritual Dagger
- **Location**: Ritual chamber altar
- **Description**: Obsidian dagger with cult symbols etched into the blade. Stained with old blood.
- **Value**: 50 gp (cursed)
- **Category**: cursed_item, weapon
- **Hidden**: false
- **Notes**: **Curse**: Bearer becomes increasingly paranoid and violent. Whispers tempt bearer to perform blood sacrifices. DC 15 Wisdom saving throw each dawn to resist compulsion. Can be cleansed by Remove Curse spell.
