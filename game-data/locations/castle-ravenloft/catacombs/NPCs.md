# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Catacombs

## Strahd von Zarovich (Final Battle)
- **CR:** 15
- **Location:** K86 (coffin) or fighting throughout catacombs
- **Behavior:** Phase 5 (Desperate) - uses all abilities, lair actions, legendary actions. Fights to the death.
- **Coffin State:** If resting in coffin during day, paralyzed and vulnerable. Can be staked without combat (instant kill).
- **Misty Escape:** If reduced to 0 HP outside coffin, transforms to mist, flees to K86. Party has 2 hours to prevent regeneration.

## Wight Guardians (K87)
- **Type:** Wight
- **CR:** 3
- **Count:** 1d6+2
- **Behavior:** Defend approach to K86. Use Life Drain, create zombie spawn from killed PCs.

## Wraith Guardians (K87, K88)
- **Type:** Wraith
- **CR:** 5
- **Count:** 1d4
- **Behavior:** Phase through walls, use Life Drain to reduce max HP. Prioritize healers.

## Vampire Spawn (Reinforcements)
- **Type:** Vampire Spawn
- **CR:** 5
- **Count:** 2d3
- **Behavior:** Arrive as reinforcements if combat lasts 5+ rounds. Strahd summons them telepathically.
