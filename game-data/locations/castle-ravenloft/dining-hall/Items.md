# Items in Castle Ravenloft - Dining Hall

## Visible Items

### Illusory Feast (K10)
- **Item ID:** illusory_feast
- **Name:** The Eternal Banquet
- **Category:** Illusion (non-item)
- **Description:** Sumptuous food and wine covering the banquet table. All illusion—turns to ash, dust, and cobwebs when touched.
- **Properties:** Permanent illusion. Detect Magic reveals illusion aura. Once touched, transformation is permanent (doesn't reform).
- **Value:** Worthless (illusion)

### Silver Candelabra (K10)
- **Item ID:** dining_hall_candelabra
- **Name:** Von Zarovich Dining Candelabra
- **Count:** 5 candelabra on table
- **Value:** 50 gp each (250 gp total)
- **Properties:** Three-armed silver holders with everburning candles
- **Weight:** 5 lbs each

### Ornate Dining Chairs (K10)
- **Item ID:** dining_chairs
- **Count:** 20 chairs + 1 throne
- **Value:** Chairs 10 gp each (200 gp), Throne 100 gp
- **Properties:** High-backed, carved dark wood, von Zarovich coat of arms
- **Curse (Throne only):** Non-Strahd sitter makes DC 15 Wisdom save or disadvantage on Charisma checks for 1 hour

### Tapestries (K10)
- **Item ID:** dining_hall_tapestries
- **Count:** 6 tapestries on walls
- **Value:** 75 gp each (450 gp total)
- **Description:** Disturbing feast imagery—hollow-eyed celebrants, plates of ash
- **Weight:** 15 lbs each (bulky)

## Hidden Items

### Poisoned Wine (If Strahd Provides Real Food)
- **Item ID:** poisoned_wine_goblet
- **Location:** Specific goblets Strahd offers to "honor" guests
- **Description:** Real wine (not illusion), but laced with poison
- **Properties:** DC 15 Constitution save or 4d6 poison damage + poisoned condition for 1 hour
- **Note:** Strahd is immune to poison, will drink from "safe" goblets while guests get poisoned ones

### Hidden Compartment in Throne
- **Item ID:** throne_compartment
- **Location:** Under throne seat cushion, DC 16 Investigation
- **Contents:**
  - Dagger +1 (Strahd's backup weapon)
  - 50 platinum pieces
  - Poison vial (Midnight Tears, ingested, DC 17 Con save or death in 12 hours)
- **Total Value:** ~750 gp

### Archer Post Arsenal (K11)
- **Item ID:** archer_post_weapons
- **Location:** In archer gallery (K11)
- **Contents:**
  - 40 crossbow bolts (10 silvered)
  - 3 light crossbows
  - 2d4 vials alchemist's fire
  - Bag of caltrops
- **Value:** ~150 gp
- **Access:** Must reach archer post via hidden stairs from upper levels

## Loot Summary
**Total Value:** ~1,100 gp (if all items collected, which is impractical—table, chairs, tapestries bulky)

**Most Valuable:**
- Tapestries (450 gp total)
- Candelabra (250 gp total)
- Throne compartment contents (750 gp)

**Most Useful:**
- Dagger +1 (from throne compartment)
- Silvered crossbow bolts (vs undead)
- Midnight Tears poison (if used against Strahd's minions)
