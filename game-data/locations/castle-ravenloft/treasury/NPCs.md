# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Treasury

## Wraith Guardian (Conditional)
- **CR:** 5
- **Present:** 30% at night
- **Behavior:** Defends vault, uses Life Drain, phases through walls

## Strahd (Occasional Visit)
- **CR:** 15
- **Visits:** 20% at night (checking on wealth)
- **Behavior:** If party caught looting, attacks immediately (Phase 4, no mercy)

## Animated Armor (Alternate Guardian)
- **CR:** 1
- **Count:** 1d4 if wraith not present
- **Behavior:** Defends vault entrance, fights to destruction
