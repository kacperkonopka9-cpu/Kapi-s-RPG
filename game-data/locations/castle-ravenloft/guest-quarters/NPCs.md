# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Guest Quarters

## Vampire Spawn Brides (Ludmilla, Anastrasya, Volenta)
- **CR:** 5 each
- **Count:** 2d3 present at night (70% chance)
- **Behavior:** Coordinate attacks, use Spider Climb, grapple/bite tactics
- **Personality:** Ludmilla (tactical), Anastrasya (seductive), Volenta (sadistic)

## Escher (Vampire Spawn Consort)
- **CR:** 5
- **Location:** Here or tower-of-strahd
- **Behavior:** Melancholic artist, devoted to Strahd, reluctant combatant

## Strahd (Occasional Visit)
- **CR:** 15
- **Visits to gaze at Tatyana's portrait:** 20% chance at night
- **Behavior:** Melancholic if interrupted gazing at portrait; may attack or depart depending on mood
