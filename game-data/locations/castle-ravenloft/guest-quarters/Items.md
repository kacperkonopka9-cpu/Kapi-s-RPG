# Items in Castle Ravenloft - Guest Quarters

## Tatyana's Portrait (K56)
- **Value:** 1,000 gp (masterwork)
- **Secret:** Hidden passage behind (DC 15 Investigation)
- **Curse:** Strahd becomes enraged if removed or damaged

## Vampire Spawn Personal Treasures
- **Location:** Individual rooms (K50-K51)
- **Per spawn:**
  - Jewelry collection (3d4 × 100 gp)
  - Art objects (2d4 × 50 gp)
  - Fine clothing (50 gp)
  - Personal diary (lore value)
- **Total:** ~2,000 gp if all collected

## Coffins (Daytime Shelters)
- **Location:** K50-K51
- **Note:** NOT true resting places (spawn regenerate elsewhere if destroyed in these coffins)
- **Loot per coffin:** 2d10 gp, personal effects

## Hidden Safe (K56)
- **Location:** Behind Tatyana portrait, DC 18 to open
- **Contents:** 500 pp, diamond necklace (2,500 gp), love letters (Strahd to Tatyana)
