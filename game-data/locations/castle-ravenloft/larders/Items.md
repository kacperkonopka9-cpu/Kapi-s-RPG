# Items in Castle Ravenloft - Larders

## Rahadin's Keys (K72)
- **Location:** Rahadin's desk or on his person
- **Contents:** Master key ring (all dungeon cells, treasury access, Strahd's chambers)
- **Value:** Tactical (provides access throughout castle)

## Prison Ledgers (K72)
- **Location:** Rahadin's office desk
- **Contents:** 300 years of prisoner records, crimes, fates
- **Lore Value:** High (reveals Strahd's history, victims, methods)

## Torture Implements
- **Location:** Throughout dungeons (K74, K75)
- **Items:** Iron maiden, rack, hot pokers, chains
- **Value:** 10-50 gp each as scrap metal
- **Note:** Evidence of atrocities

## Cell Loot
- **Location:** Various cells (K73)
- **Contents per cell:**
  - 2d10 sp (prisoners' last possessions)
  - 10% chance: Small gemstone (10-50 gp)
  - 5% chance: Magic item (potion, scroll, +1 weapon)

## Rahadin's Personal Effects (K72)
- **Location:** Office safe, DC 18 to pick or open with keys
- **Contents:**
  - 300 pp
  - Scimitar +1 (backup weapon)
  - Cloak of Elvenkind
  - Letters from Strahd (orders, commendations)
- **Total Value:** ~2,000 gp

## Cyrus's Hovel Loot
- **Location:** Mongrelfolk quarters
- **Contents:** Worthless junk, but includes castle map (hand-drawn, shows secret passages)
