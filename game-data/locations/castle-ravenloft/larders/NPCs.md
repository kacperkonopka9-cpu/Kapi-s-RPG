# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Larders

## Rahadin (Chamberlain)
- **CR:** 10
- **Location:** Office (K72), 60% present
- **Behavior:** Cold, efficient, utterly loyal to Strahd. Deathly Choir aura (DC 15 Wis or frightened).
- **Combat:** Dual scimitars, high mobility, defends office fiercely.

## Cyrus Belview (Mongrelfolk Servant)
- **CR:** 1/4
- **Location:** Hovel near dungeons
- **Behavior:** Fearful, subservient, may provide information if treated kindly
- **Non-combat:** Knows castle layout, guard schedules, Strahd's habits

## Vampire Spawn Guards
- **CR:** 5
- **Count:** 1d3 on patrol
- **Behavior:** Feed on prisoners, guard cells, report to Rahadin

## Prisoners (Various)
- **Living Prisoners:** 1d3 Barovian commoners (CR 0), potential rescue
- **Zombie Prisoners:** 1d4 zombies (CR 1/4), former prisoners risen as undead
- **Special Prisoner:** Possible quest NPC (DM choice) - provides information or side quest
