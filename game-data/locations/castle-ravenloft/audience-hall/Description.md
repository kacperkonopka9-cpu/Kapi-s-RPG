# Castle Ravenloft - Audience Hall

## Overview
The Audience Hall is Strahd's throne room where he holds court, receives petitioners, and makes formal judgments. Rahadin often attends here. The throne radiates power and menace.

## Initial Description
You enter a grand hall (70 feet long, 50 feet wide, 30-foot vaulted ceiling) dominated by an ornate throne on a raised dais. The throne is carved from black stone with red velvet cushions, the von Zarovich coat of arms emblazoned above. Tattered banners hang from the ceiling. Guard posts flank the throne.

## Return Description
The throne room waits. The throne sits empty or occupied by Strahd depending on his presence. This is where formal power is displayed and judgments rendered.

## Time-Based Variants
### Morning/Day
Hall is typically empty during day. Throne waits silent.

### Night
Strahd may hold court here (30% chance). Rahadin present (60% chance). Formal encounters, roleplay opportunities.

## Rooms and Areas
### K25: Audience Hall (Main)
Central throne room with throne, guard posts, banners.

### K26-K30: Adjacent Chambers
Guards' posts, king's hall, king's apartment (unused), treasury access points. Minor treasures (3d10 gp per room, art objects).

## Points of Interest
- **Strahd's Throne** - Black stone throne with von Zarovich symbols
- **Guard Posts** - Flanking positions for defenders
- **Treasury Access** - Passages connecting to treasure vault
- **Formal Court Space** - Where Strahd receives petitioners

## Atmosphere
Formal power on display. The throne represents Strahd's authority as count and ruler, even though his domain is cursed and his subjects trapped.
