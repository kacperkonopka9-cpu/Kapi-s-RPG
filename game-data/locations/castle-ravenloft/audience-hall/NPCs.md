# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Audience Hall

## Strahd von Zarovich
- **CR:** 15
- **Present:** 30% at night (holding court)
- **Behavior:** Formal, regal, passing judgment on petitioners (party may be treated as petitioners)

## Rahadin
- **CR:** 10
- **Present:** 60% (attending Strahd or managing affairs)
- **Behavior:** Formal chamberlain, announces visitors, defends throne

## Animated Armor Guards
- **CR:** 1
- **Count:** 1d4+1 in guard posts
- **Behavior:** Activate if throne threatened or Strahd commands
