# Items in Castle Ravenloft

## Overview
Castle Ravenloft contains vast treasures accumulated over centuries of conquest, plunder, and dark magic. The specific locations of legendary artifacts are determined by the Tarokka reading (Story 4-16), but certain treasures and items are always found in the castle.

## Legendary Artifacts (Location Variable)

### Potential Artifact Placements
Based on Tarokka card reading, one or more of these legendary items may be hidden in Castle Ravenloft:

- **Sunsword** - Sentient radiant longsword, bane of vampires (Tarokka placement)
- **Holy Symbol of Ravenkind** - Powerful holy relic with vampire-warding abilities (Tarokka placement)
- **Tome of Strahd** - Strahd's personal journal revealing his history and vulnerabilities (Tarokka placement)

**Note:** These items' exact locations are determined by Tarokka reading system (Story 4-16) and may be placed in various sub-locations within the castle.

## Castle-Wide Treasures

### General Loot Categories

**Art Objects & Valuables:**
- Ancient tapestries depicting Zarovich family history (100-500 gp each)
- Paintings of Barovian nobility in gilded frames (50-300 gp each)
- Marble busts and statues (25-150 gp each)
- Crystal chandeliers (200-800 gp, fragile)
- Ornate furniture (varies, 10-500 gp)

**Coins & Gems:**
- Ancient Barovian coinage (platinum, gold, electrum, silver, copper)
- Cut gemstones scattered throughout castle (10-1000 gp each)
- Jewelry pieces (rings, necklaces, bracelets, 25-2000 gp)

**Books & Scrolls:**
- Rare tomes on necromancy, vampirism, and dark magic (25-500 gp each)
- Spell scrolls of various levels (see specific sub-locations)
- Historical records and family genealogies
- Strahd's personal library in Tower study

**Magic Items (General):**
- +1 weapons and armor (various types throughout castle)
- Potions of healing, restoration, and other effects
- Wands, rings, and wondrous items (see specific sub-locations for details)

## Sub-Location Specific Loot

**Detailed item placements are found in each sub-location's Items.md file:**

- **Treasury (K41)** - Main treasure hoard: 50,000 cp, 10,000 sp, 1,000 ep, 5,000 gp, 500 pp, gems, jewelry, magic items
- **Dining Hall (K10)** - Fine silverware, crystal goblets, serving dishes (2,000 gp total)
- **Chapel (K15)** - Desecrated holy items, religious texts, saint relics (corrupted)
- **Tower of Strahd (K20, K45-K48)** - Arcane library, spell scrolls, research materials, Tome of Strahd (if placed)
- **Catacombs (K84-K88)** - Burial treasures, weapons, armor, family heirlooms
- **Guest Quarters** - Personal effects of prisoners, hidden caches
- **Larders** - Torture implements, keys, mundane supplies
- **Audience Hall** - Throne, royal regalia, court treasures

## Quest Items

**Key Quest-Related Items:**
- **Icon of Ravenloft** - Family heirloom revealing Strahd's history
- **Brazier Coals** - From Chapel, needed for special rituals
- **Keys to Various Locked Doors** - Found on guards, in hidden locations
- **Letters and Journals** - Revealing castle history, NPC motivations
- **Sergei's Sword** - In catacombs, symbolic importance

## Cursed Items Warning

Many items in Castle Ravenloft are cursed or trapped. Exercise caution when claiming treasures:
- Mirrors may be portals or magical traps
- Books may contain dangerous knowledge or summon entities
- Weapons may be bonded to undead owners
- Jewelry may bear curses or tracking enchantments

## Equipment & Supplies

**Practical Items Available:**
- Torches and oil (in storage areas)
- Rations (spoiled, inedible)
- Rope and climbing gear (in guard rooms)
- Holy water (2-3 vials in Chapel, rare)
- Wooden stakes (hidden in Chapel cellar)
- Garlic and mirrors (previous adventurers' gear)
