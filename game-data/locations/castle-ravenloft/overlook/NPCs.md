# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Overlook

## Gargoyles
- **CR:** 2
- **Count:** 2d4 perched on parapets
- **Behavior:** Attack intruders, use flight advantage, attempt to push enemies off edges

## Strahd (Retreat Location)
- **CR:** 15
- **Present:** 40% at night (surveying domain) OR if wounded in combat (tactical retreat)
- **Behavior:** Uses Misty Step to reach tower peaks, fights from height advantage, forces enemies to deal with winds and falls

## Vampire Spawn (Reinforcements)
- **CR:** 5
- **Count:** 1d3 if Strahd calls for aid
- **Behavior:** Use Spider Climb to scale walls, attack from unexpected angles
