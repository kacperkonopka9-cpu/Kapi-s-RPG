# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Hall of Bones

## Skeletal Guardians
- **Type:** Skeleton
- **CR:** 1/4
- **Count:** 1d4+2
- **Behavior:** Activate when party enters, attack in formation

## Wights (Reinforcements)
- **Type:** Wight
- **CR:** 3
- **Count:** 1d3 if combat lasts 4+ rounds
- **Behavior:** Emerge from guard chambers, use Life Drain
