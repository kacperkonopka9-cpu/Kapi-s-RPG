# Items in Castle Ravenloft - Tower of Strahd

## Strahd's Spellbooks (3 Volumes)
- **Location:** Study desk and bookshelves (K37)
- **Contents:** 80+ spells across all levels (1st-9th)
- **Value:** 5,000 gp to wizards (spell copying value)
- **Trap:** Taking without permission triggers animated bookshelves (1d4, CR 2 each)

## Crystal Ball
- **Location:** Study desk (K37)
- **Properties:** Strahd's scrying focus
- **Trap:** If touched by non-Strahd, explodes (DC 15 Dex, 6d6 force damage, 10-foot radius)
- **Value:** 1,000 gp if safely extracted (requires Dispel Magic DC 17)

## Portrait of Tatyana
- **Location:** Above fireplace (K37)
- **Properties:** Acts as scrying sensor (Strahd can watch through it)
- **Value:** 750 gp (masterwork painting)
- **Secret:** Behind portrait—hidden safe with 200 pp, diamond (1,000 gp), love letters to Tatyana

## Heart of Sorrow (K20)
- **Item ID:** heart_of_sorrow
- **Category:** Artifact
- **Properties:**
  - AC 17, 50 HP
  - Absorbs first 50 damage dealt to Strahd each round
  - Destruction ends protection permanently
  - Strahd aware if attacked
- **Value:** Priceless (destroying it weakens Strahd significantly)
