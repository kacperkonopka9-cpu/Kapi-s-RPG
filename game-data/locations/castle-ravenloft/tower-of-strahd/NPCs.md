# NPCs in Castle Ravenloft - Tower of Strahd

## Count Strahd von Zarovich (80% Present at Night)
- **CR:** 15
- **Location:** Study (K37) or Private Chambers
- **Behavior:** Extremely hostile to intruders in his sanctum. Phase 4-5 combat (full engagement or desperate).
- **Dialogue:** "You DARE enter my personal chambers? Your deaths will be exquisite and prolonged."

### Combat Notes
- Fights to defend Heart of Sorrow with full legendary actions and lair actions
- Uses study terrain (teleports behind bookshelves, Misty Steps to tower peaks)
- If Heart destroyed, becomes enraged (advantage on attacks, disadvantage on ability checks due to pain/fury)
- Retreats to coffin only if reduced below 10 HP

## Escher (Vampire Spawn Consort)
- **Type:** Vampire Spawn
- **CR:** 5
- **Location:** Private chambers or study (70% present when Strahd is here)
- **Behavior:** Melancholic artist, devoted to Strahd, defends tower
- **Dialogue:** "The master values his privacy. Leave now, or face consequences beyond your comprehension."

### Combat Notes
- Supports Strahd in combat (flanking, grapple/bite tactics)
- Uses Spider Climb to attack from ceiling
- If Strahd retreats, Escher holds party (buys time for master)

## Animated Bookshelves (Magical Guardians)
- **Type:** Animated Objects
- **CR:** 2 each
- **Count:** 1d4 shelves animate if intruders touch spellbooks without permission
- **Behavior:** Slam intruders, attempt to crush against walls

## Spectral Guardian (Heart of Sorrow Chamber, K20)
- **Type:** Unique Entity
- **CR:** 7
- **Status:** Manifests if party attempts to destroy Heart
- **Behavior:** Defends Heart, uses necrotic attacks
- **HP:** 75, AC 15
- **Special:** Reformed from destroyed life-forces absorbed by Heart over centuries
