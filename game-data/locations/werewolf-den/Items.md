# Items in Werewolf Den

## Main Cavern

### General Items
- **Animal Pelts:** Various furs worth 2-10 gp each (20 pelts total, 120 gp value)
- **Bone Decorations:** Carved bones and skulls, no monetary value but disturbing
- **Crude Weapons:** Clubs, spears, axes worth 1-5 gp each (15 weapons total)
- **Coinage (Scattered):** Looted from victims, 78 gp, 145 sp, 220 cp

## Kiril's Chamber

### Kiril's Possessions
- **Trophy Collection:** Human skulls, weapons from defeated enemies, disturbing displays
- **Coinage:** 240 gp, 180 sp (looted from raids)
- **Stolen Jewelry:** From kidnapping victims, 6 pieces worth 15-50 gp each (190 gp total)
- **Kiril's Greataxe:** Large silvered greataxe, 2d12 damage, worth 500 gp (Kiril uses this in human form)
- **Human Leather Vest:** Disturbing armor Kiril wears, provides AC 12, no one wants this
- **Letter from Strahd:** Sealed letter acknowledging Kiril's "service" in raiding settlements, proves connection to vampire lord

## Individual Den Chambers

### Werewolf Personal Effects
- **Clothing and Supplies:** Various gear worth 5-15 gp per chamber (80 gp total from 8 chambers)
- **Food Stores:** Dried meats (some human, disturbing), smoked fish, stolen provisions
- **Weapons:** Mostly mundane, 1 silvered dagger found (50 gp, ironic given owners)
- **Hidden Coins:** 120 gp total scattered among various chambers

## Emil's Prison Cell

### Emil's Possessions (If Freed)
- **Worn Clothing:** Simple mountain clothing
- **Makeshift Journal:** Carved messages on cave wall documenting imprisonment
- **Hidden Escape Plan:** Map scratched into stone showing back tunnel exit

## Children's Prison

### Children's Belongings
- **Personal Items:** Small toys, torn clothing, copper pieces (sentimental value only)
- **Evidence of Origins:** Items that can identify victims and allow return to families
- **Lockets and Keepsakes:** 3 items worth 5-10 gp each to return to families

## Quest Items

### Letter from Strahd (Kiril's Chamber)
- **Content:** Acknowledges Kiril's "useful service" in destabilizing Krezk through raids
- **Implication:** Proves werewolf raids are part of Strahd's broader strategy
- **Value:** Critical evidence of Strahd's manipulation of various factions

### Children's Identification Items
- **Ilya's Wooden Horse:** Toy that can be returned to Krezk family
- **Marta's Locket:** Contains portrait of her parents
- **Dimi's Cap:** Distinctive green cap his mother made

### Emil's Cell Map
- **Value:** Shows back tunnel escape route
- **Tactical Use:** Allows party to bypass main entrance or create flanking routes

## Loot Ethics and Practical Considerations

### Stolen Property
- Much of the wealth here was looted from victims
- Jewelry and personal items should ideally be returned to families
- Taking everything is theft from grieving families
- Returning items earns significant gratitude from Krezk

### Silvered Weapons
- Extremely valuable in Barovia (werewolves, vampires)
- Cache represents 325 gp worth of anti-lycanthrope weapons
- Essential for fighting werewolves without magic

### Pack Treasure
- If Emil restored to leadership, he allows party to take raided goods
- He insists children's belongings be returned to families
- If Kiril defeated, pack's treasure is legitimate loot

### Total Available Loot
- **Coinage:** ~940 gp total (if taking everything)
- **Weapons:** ~1075 gp (mostly silvered weapons)
- **Jewelry/Valuables:** ~530 gp
- **Potions:** 200 gp worth
- **Total:** ~2745 gp value, but much should be returned to victims' families
