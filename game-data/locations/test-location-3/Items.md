# Items in Ancient Library

## Available Items

### Circulation Desk (Purchasing/Copying Services)
- **Research Access Fee (Second Floor):** 5 sp per day (grants access to specialized archives)
- **Book Copying Service:** 1 gp per 10 pages (scribe will copy requested passages, takes 1 day)
- **Parchment (10 sheets):** 1 sp
- **Ink Vial (black):** 1 sp
- **Quill Pen:** 2 cp
- **Wax Seal Kit:** 5 sp (wax and personalized stamp for official documents)

### Reading Materials (For Reference, Not Removal Without Checkout)
- **General Knowledge Tome:** 0 cp to read (history, geography, general subjects)
- **Specialized Treatise:** 5 sp research fee (second floor access required—magic theory, rare history, natural philosophy)
- **Restricted Text:** Special permission required (third floor—arcane secrets, dangerous knowledge)

## Hidden Items (Requires Investigation)
- **Forgotten Spellbook:** DC 20 Investigation in the second floor archives, hidden between larger tomes. Contains 3 1st-level wizard spells (player choice of common spells). Previous owner was a careless scholar who forgot to return it decades ago. Master Aldwyn would be pleased to have it returned for proper cataloging, though he might allow the finder to copy one spell as a reward.
