# Argynvostholt - Items

## Quest Items

### Argynvost's Skull
```yaml
itemId: argynvost_skull
name: "Skull of Argynvost"
category: quest_item
location: chapel_altar
value: priceless
hidden: false
requires: vladimir_permission_or_combat
```

**Properties**: Silver dragon skull, 15 feet long. Key to lighting the beacon. Must be returned to mausoleum and ritual performed.

**Quest**: Returning skull to mausoleum and lighting beacon summons Argynvost's spirit, granting party blessing against Strahd (+1 AC and saves vs. Strahd's abilities).

## Notes
