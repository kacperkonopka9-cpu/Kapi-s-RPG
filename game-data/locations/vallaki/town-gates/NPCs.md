# Town Gates - NPCs

## Gate Captain
**NPC ID:** `gate_captain`
**Type:** Human Veteran (CR 3)
**Description:** Grizzled veteran who takes security seriously. Less concerned with festivals than with actual threats.
**Dialogue:** "State your business in Vallaki. And before you ask—yes, you'll be expected to attend the festivals. Baron's orders. But between you and me? Better a forced smile than whatever's out there in the mist."

## Gate Guards (Day Shift)
**NPC ID:** `gate_guards_day`
**Type:** Human Guards (CR 0.5)
**Count:** 4 guards (2 per gate during day)
**Description:** Guards who inspect travelers, check for weapons, assess attitudes
**Dialogue:** "Welcome to Vallaki, traveler. You look tired—there's an inn called the Blue Water, east along the main road. Just remember: the Baron expects enthusiasm from everyone. Keep smiling, and you'll do fine."

## Gate Guards (Night Shift)
**NPC ID:** `gate_guards_night`
**Type:** Human Guards (CR 0.5)
**Count:** 6 guards (3 per gate during night, gates CLOSED)
**Description:** Nervous night watch, alert for threats from beyond the walls
**Dialogue:** "Gates don't open until dawn. Not for anyone. Not for any reason. The Baron's law, and it's kept us alive this long."

## Tower Watchmen
**NPC ID:** `tower_watchmen`
**Type:** Human Guards (CR 0.5)
**Count:** 2 per tower (4 total)
**Description:** Crossbow-armed sentries scanning approaches to town
**Dialogue:** "Movement on the road! Wait... just more refugees from Barovia. Poor bastards."
