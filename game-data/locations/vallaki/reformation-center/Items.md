# Reformation Center - Items

## Guard Armory
**Item ID:** `reformation_armory`
**Value:** 50 gp total
**Category:** equipment
**Description:** Spears (6), shortswords (8), light crossbows (4), leather armor sets (6). Standard town watch equipment.
**Availability:** Locked cabinet, DC 15 Thieves' Tools to open

## Duty Roster
**Item ID:** `guard_duty_roster`
**Category:** document
**Description:** Guard rotation schedule. Reveals patterns: fewer guards during festivals, night watch is skeleton crew (2 guards only).

## Cell Keys
**Item ID:** `reformation_cell_keys`
**Category:** mundane
**Description:** Iron key ring holding keys to all cells. Carried by Captain of the Guard. Can be stolen (DC 18 Sleight of Hand) or obtained by defeating/persuading captain.

## Prisoner Effects
**Item ID:** `prisoner_belongings`
**Value:** 2d6 gp total
**Category:** mundane
**Description:** Confiscated belongings of current prisoners: coins, simple tools, personal items. Stored in locked chest (DC 12 to pick).
