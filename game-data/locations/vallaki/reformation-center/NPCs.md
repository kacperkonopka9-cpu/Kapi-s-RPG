# Reformation Center - NPCs

## Captain of the Guard (Izek's Lieutenant)
**NPC ID:** `reformation_captain`
**Type:** Human Guard (CR 1)
**Description:** Weary veteran who enforces Baron's edicts without belief or enthusiasm
**Dialogue:** "You're free to go... for now. The Baron expects to see you smiling at the next festival. Don't disappoint him."

## Town Watch Guards
**NPC ID:** `town_watch_guards`
**Type:** Human Guards (CR 0.5)
**Count:** 2d4 guards on duty at any time
**Description:** Young guards uncomfortable with their role as enforcers of happiness
**Dialogue:** "Just... just keep smiling. It's easier for everyone that way."

## Prisoners (Current Malcontents)
**NPC ID:** `current_prisoners`
**Type:** Commoners
**Count:** 1d6 prisoners at any given time
**Description:** Townspeople imprisoned for insufficient enthusiasm. Crimes include: failing to applaud loudly enough, expressing doubt, looking sad at a festival.
**Dialogue:** "I only wanted to mourn my mother's passing. Is that so wrong? The Baron says grief is malcontentedness. Three days in here, and I've learned to smile through anything."
