# Town of Vallaki - Items

## Major Town Treasures

### Legendary Artifact Potential Locations
Based on Madam Eva's Tarokka reading, one or more legendary artifacts may be hidden in Vallaki:

- **Sunsword**: Potential location if Tarokka indicates "A wounded elf has what you seek" (Rictavio/Van Richten at Blue Water Inn)
- **Holy Symbol of Ravenkind**: Potential location if Tarokka indicates "Look to the mother's grave" (Church of St. Andral)
- **Tome of Strahd**: Potential location if Tarokka indicates "Seek knowledge in the mansion of a great leader" (Burgomaster's Mansion)

*Note: Actual artifact placement determined by Tarokka reading system (Story 4-16)*

## Quest Items

**Item ID:** `st_andrals_bones`
**Name:** St. Andral's Bones
**Category:** quest
**Value:** Priceless (sacred)
**Description:** The blessed bones of St. Andral, the ancient cleric who consecrated the church. While in the church, these bones create a barrier preventing undead from entering. Their theft would leave Vallaki vulnerable to vampire attack.
**Location:** Should be in church-of-st-andral crypt, but stolen by coffin maker (see St. Andral's Feast quest)
**Hidden:** DC 13 Investigation to discover theft, DC 18 Investigation to track to coffin maker

## Magic Item Availability

**Vallaki Market (Limited):**
- Potions of Healing: 50 gp each (2d4 available)
- Holy Water: 25 gp per vial (1d6 available from church)
- Silvered Weapons: Double normal cost (special order, 1 week wait)
- Spell Scrolls: Rare, occasionally available from traveling merchants (1d4 per month, levels 1-2 only)

*Note: Vallaki is the largest trading hub in Barovia. If magic items exist in this accursed land, they're most likely to be found or commissioned here.*
