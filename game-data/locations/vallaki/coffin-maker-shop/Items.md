# Coffin Maker's Shop - Items

## St. Andral's Bones (QUEST CRITICAL)
**Item ID:** `st_andrals_bones`
**Value:** Priceless (holy relic)
**Category:** quest_item
**Description:** Sacred bones of St. Andral, stolen from the church. While removed from consecrated ground, the church loses its protection against vampires. Hidden in false bottom of workbench.
**Hidden:** Yes, DC 15 Investigation to find false panel in workbench
**Quest Impact:** Returning bones to Father Lucian prevents St. Andral's Feast attack event. Failing to return within 3 days triggers vampire spawn attack on church.
