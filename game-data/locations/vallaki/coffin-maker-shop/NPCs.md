# Coffin Maker's Shop - NPCs

## Henrik van der Voort
**NPC ID:** `henrik_van_der_voort`
**Type:** Human Commoner (CR 0)
**Description:** Terrified coffin maker, coerced by Strahd's agents into hiding stolen bones and harboring vampire spawn
**Personality:** Nervous, guilty, terrified. Wants to confess but fears retribution from the vampire who threatened him.
**Dialogue:** 
- "Just browsing for a coffin? Bit early for that, I hope! Heh... heh..."
- (If pressed about upstairs): "My living quarters! Very messy, not fit for visitors!"
- (If confronted with evidence): "I didn't want to! He said he'd kill me! Kill my family! I didn't know what the bones were for, I swear!"

**Investigation Notes:**
- DC 13 Insight: Henrik is hiding something and terrified
- DC 15 Intimidation or Persuasion: Henrik breaks down and confesses about the bones
- If shown St. Andral's symbol or questioned about church: DC 12 Insight reveals he knows something

## Vampire Spawn Nest (Upstairs)
**NPC ID:** `vampire_spawn_nest`
**Type:** Vampire Spawn (CR 5)
**Count:** 6 vampire spawn
**Description:** Six vampire spawn "gifts" from Strahd, ordered to remain hidden until St. Andral's Feast is triggered (when bones are not returned to church within 3 days)
**Status:** Dormant unless players enter upstairs, or event triggers their departure for the church attack

**Combat Notes:**
- Spawn are ORDERED to remain hidden until signal
- If players enter upstairs: spawn attack to protect their hiding place
- Spawn flee via windows if clearly outmatched (CR 5 each, but 6 of them = deadly for level 3-5 party)
