## Milivoj
**NPC ID:** `milivoj`
**Type:** Human Commoner (CR 0)
**Role:** Church altar boy, unwitting accomplice to bones theft

**Description:** A teenage boy with hollow cheeks and desperate eyes. Poor family forces him to take any work, including burying the dead. Desperate for money to feed siblings.

**Dialogue:** "I... I just needed the money. My family is starving. I didn't know what it would mean!"

**Quest Role:** Stole St. Andral's bones for Henrik the coffin maker (30 gp payment). Will confess if questioned with DC 12 Intimidation or DC 10 Persuasion.

**Location:** Church during day, town square in evening
