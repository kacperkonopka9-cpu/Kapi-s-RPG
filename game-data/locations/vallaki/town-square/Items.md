# Town Square - Items

## Festival Decorations
**Item ID:** `town_square_decorations`
**Value:** 2d6 gp materials
**Category:** treasure
**Description:** Current festival's ribbons, banners, painted props

## Market Goods
**Item ID:** `town_square_market`
**Value:** Variable
**Category:** treasure
**Description:** Basic supplies available from shops: rope, rations, torches, common tools
**Availability:** During day, shops open

## Stocks Equipment
**Item ID:** `punishment_stocks`
**Category:** mundane
**Description:** Wooden stocks for public punishment. Can be picked (DC 15 Thieves' Tools) to free prisoners.
