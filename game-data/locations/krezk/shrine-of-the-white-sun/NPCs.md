# Shrine of the White Sun - NPCs

### Father Andrei
See parent location for full profile. At the shrine, Father Andrei is accessible for blessings, guidance, and religious services. Can cast minor healing spells (Cure Wounds, Lesser Restoration) once per day as favor to faithful or helpful adventurers.

**Services Offered**:
- Blessings (+1 to saving throws for 24 hours)
- Minor healing (if party has helped village)
- Religious counsel and lore
- Funeral rites
- Wedding ceremonies
- Blessing holy water

### Worshippers
- **Count**: 20-40 villagers attend each service
- **Behavior**: Devout, fearful, desperate for divine protection
- **Atmosphere**: Prayers sound more like pleas than praise

**Notable Worshippers**:
- Dmitri and Anna (when not at Ilya's bedside)
- Village elders
- Families seeking protection
- Those who have lost loved ones
