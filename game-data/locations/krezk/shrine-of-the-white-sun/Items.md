# Shrine of the White Sun - Items

### Prayer Books
- **itemId**: `shrine_prayer_books`
- **Name**: Collection of Prayer Books
- **Description**: Hand-copied prayers to the Morninglord, hymn books, religious texts
- **Value**: 50 gp as collection
- **Note**: Taking these is sacrilege

### Incense and Candles
- **itemId**: `shrine_incense`
- **Name**: Incense and Holy Candles
- **Description**: Used in religious ceremonies
- **Value**: 10 gp
- **Category**: consumable

### Donation Offerings
- **itemId**: `shrine_offerings`
- **Name**: Villagers' Donations
- **Description**: Copper and silver coins in offering bowl. Villagers give what little they can.
- **Value**: 15 sp, 45 cp
- **Note**: Taking this is theft from the poor—severe trust penalty

### Holy Symbol of the Morninglord
- **itemId**: `shrine_holy_symbol`
- **Name**: Brass Sunburst Symbol
- **Description**: Altar's central holy symbol, blessed by Father Andrei
- **Category**: equipment
- **Properties**: Functions as holy symbol for clerics/paladins
- **Value**: 25 gp (but stealing it is unthinkable)

### Father Andrei's Personal Items
- **itemId**: `father_andrei_possessions`
- **Location**: His quarters
- **Description**: Simple robes, walking stick, personal prayer journal, wooden holy symbol
- **Value**: Minimal
- **Note**: Journal contains his private crisis of faith—reading provides insight into village's desperation
