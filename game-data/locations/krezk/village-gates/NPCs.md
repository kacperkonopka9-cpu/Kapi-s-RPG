# Village Gates - NPCs

### Boris (Gatekeeper)
See parent location for full profile. At the gates, Boris is on duty, suspicious, and will not let party enter without Burgomaster's approval or compelling reason (wine delivery, urgent healing).

**Entry Requirements**:
- Bring wine from Wizard of Wines
- Claim ability to heal Ilya (must seem credible)
- Be vouched for by Dmitri or trusted NPC
- Force entry (turns village hostile)

### Gate Guards (4 on duty, 8 total)
- **Type**: Human (Guard)
- **CR**: 1/8
- **Equipment**: Chain mail, longswords, light crossbows
- **Behavior**: Follow Boris's orders. Loyal to Krezk. Will defend gate to the death.

**Guard Rotation**: Guards work 8-hour shifts (06:00-14:00, 14:00-22:00, 22:00-06:00). Brief vulnerability during shift changes.
