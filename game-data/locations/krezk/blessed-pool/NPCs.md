# Blessed Pool - NPCs

### Sergei's Spirit
- **npcId**: `sergei_spirit`
- **Type**: Spirit/Ghost (good-aligned)
- **Manifestation**: Conditional (appears when worthy soul brought to pool or Ireena visits)

**Appearance**: Translucent figure of handsome young man in noble clothing. Radiates warmth and light. Face is kind, sorrowful.

**Dialogue**: "The curse... Tatyana reborn... If she comes to me willingly, I can save her from him. But she must choose."

**Role**: Sergei is Strahd's brother, murdered on his wedding day. His spirit lingers at this blessed place. Can offer resurrection for worthy souls, guidance about Barovia's curse, or salvation for Ireena/Tatyana.

### Father Andrei (Visits)
See parent/shrine locations for full profile. Comes to pool weekly to bless it and maintain offerings.

### Pilgrims (Occasional)
Desperate villagers who come to pray at the pool, seeking miracles.
