# Blessed Pool - Items

### Enhanced Holy Water
- **itemId**: `blessed_pool_holy_water`
- **Name**: Holy Water from Blessed Pool
- **Description**: Crystal-clear water more potent than standard holy water
- **Effect**: 2d6 radiant damage to undead/fiends, cures diseases and neutralizes poison when drunk
- **Category**: consumable
- **Harvestable**: Yes (can fill flasks/vials)
- **Quantity**: Unlimited (spring-fed)
