- **Drowned Treasure:** DC 15 Investigation along the pool's shore. Remains of old campsite that slid into the lake years ago. ## Special Quest Items

### Arabelle's Doll (If Quest Active)
- **Description:** Small cloth doll found if searching for missing Arabelle. Located at Tser Pool shore (DC 12 Investigation) or where she was last seen. Proves she was at the pool and suggests she either fell in or was taken. Returning this to Luvash before finding Arabelle causes him great distress but confirms the quest's urgency.

### Tarokka Prophecy Results (After Reading)
- **Prophecy Card Set:** After receiving Tarokka reading, party gains metaphysical "knowledge" of three locations marked by fate: one for Holy Symbol of Ravenkind, one for Sun Sword, one for Strahd's final confrontation location. Also reveals one ally who will aid them. These aren't physical items but campaign-critical information.

## Notes

### Trading with Vistani
- Vistani merchants are shrewd but honest
- Prices are standard D&D 5e values, no markup for being in Barovia
- Will not accept stolen goods or items taken from Vistani
- Building trust with camp (completing quests, showing respect) may unlock special items or discounts

### Madam Eva's Services
- First Tarokka reading is free and quest-critical (reveals campaign locations)
- Subsequent readings cost 10 gp and provide personal guidance
- She will not read for those who show disrespect or bad faith
- Her readings are always true but often cryptic until events unfold

### Restricted Items
- Vistani will not sell poisoned weapons or overtly evil items
- Rare magic items may become available after earning significant trust
- Weapons effective against Strahd (holy weapons, special materials) are not in general inventory but might be "found" via hidden items if party proves worthy
