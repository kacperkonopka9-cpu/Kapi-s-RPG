# Items in Van Richten's Tower

## Library (Second Floor)

### Books and Tomes
- **Van Richten's Guide to Vampires:** Comprehensive vampire hunting manual worth 500 gp, contains mechanical advantages against vampires (advantage on checks to identify vampire weaknesses)
- **Encyclopaedia Vampirica:** Reference work worth 200 gp
- **Barovian History Collection:** 15 books documenting Barovia's past, worth 300 gp total
- **Religious Texts:** Prayer books and holy writings worth 50 gp
- **Arcane References:** Books on necromancy and undead magic worth 150 gp

### Van Richten's Journal
- **Location:** Reading desk, obviously important
- **Description:** Leather-bound journal documenting Van Richten's investigation of Strahd. Reveals his disguise as "Rictavio" in Vallaki, his plans, and his analysis of Strahd's weaknesses.
- **Value:** Priceless information, reveals Van Richten's location and identity
- **Contains:** Observations on Strahd's behavior, potential weaknesses, theories about the curse, and tactical plans

## Laboratory (Third Floor)

### Alchemical Equipment
- **Alchemist's Supplies:** Worth 50 gp
- **Preserved Specimens:** Various monster parts worth 100 gp to collectors
- **Vampire Fang Collection:** 12 fangs from defeated vampires worth 200 gp total
- **Holy Water (10 vials):** 25 gp each, 250 gp total
- **Silvered Weapon Oil:** 3 applications, makes weapon count as silvered for 1 hour, worth 50 gp each

### Experimental Weapons
- **Enhanced Wooden Stakes:** 12 stakes treated with holy water and blessed, advantage on attack rolls against vampires, worth 10 gp each
- **Garlic Paste (Concentrated):** 5 jars, forces vampires within 10 feet to make DC 14 Constitution save or be repelled, worth 25 gp each

## Living Quarters (Fourth Floor)

### Personal Effects
- **Spare Clothes:** Van Richten's traveling gear worth 10 gp
- **Locket with Portrait:** Contains faded image of Van Richten's deceased wife and child (killed by vampire, motivation for his life's work), priceless to him
- **Prayer Books:** Dedicated to Morninglord and other gods, worth 20 gp
- **Weapons Maintenance Kit:** Tools worth 15 gp

### Hidden Strongbox (DC 16 Investigation)
- **300 gp in various coinage**
- **Silvered Shortsword +1:** Worth 1000 gp, effective against lycanthropes and vampires
- **Ring of Mind Shielding:** Protects against telepathy and scrying, worth 4000 gp
- **Scroll of Protection from Undead:** Single-use, worth 200 gp

## Observatory (Fifth Floor)

### Intelligence Materials
- **Maps of Barovia:** Marked with Strahd sighting locations and encounter reports, worth 100 gp
- **Telescope:** Quality instrument worth 100 gp
- **Observation Journals:** Logs of Strahd's movements and patterns, valuable for planning confrontation
- **Tactical Plans:** Van Richten's strategies for fighting Strahd, including contingency plans

### Hidden Compartment (DC 18 Investigation)
- **Tome of Strahd:** One of three campaign artifacts! Strahd's personal journal revealing his past, motivations, and hints about weaknesses. Priceless artifact essential for campaign.
- **Letter from Ireena:** Correspondence revealing Van Richten knows about Ireena and her connection to Tatyana
- **Sunblade Schematic:** Designs for reconstructing the legendary Sun Sword if found broken

- **Silvered Crossbow Bolts:** 40 bolts worth 10 gp total

- **Disguises and False Papers:** Van Richten's identity documents for various aliases worth 30 gp
- **Travel Rations:** 2 weeks' worth worth 10 gp
- **Additional Journal:** Backup copy of Van Richten's observations worth 200 gp

## Quest Items

### Van Richten's Journal (Reveals Rictavio Identity)
- **Critical Discovery:** Reveals Van Richten is disguised as Rictavio in Vallaki
- **Tactical Value:** Contains Strahd analysis and hunting strategies
- **Ethical Dilemma:** Reading without permission violates Van Richten's privacy, but information could save lives

### Tome of Strahd (Campaign Artifact)
- **One of Three Key Items:** Along with Holy Symbol of Ravenkind and Sun Sword
- **Content:** Strahd's personal journal, revealing his transformation, Tatyana history, and psychological weaknesses
- **Game Effect:** Grants insight into Strahd's motivations and potential weaknesses
- **Location Reveal:** Tarokka reading determines if Tome is here or elsewhere

### Sunblade Schematic
- **Value:** If party finds broken Sun Sword, this schematic allows repair/reconstruction
- **Requires:** Master smith, holy ritual, and rare materials
- **Connection:** Links to broader Sun Sword quest
