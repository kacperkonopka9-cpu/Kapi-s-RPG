# Events in Market Square

## Scheduled Events

### Midday Market Peak
- **Trigger:** Daily at 12:00 (noon)
- **Type:** Social/Commerce Event
- **Effect:** The market reaches its busiest point. Maximum number of merchants and customers present. All stalls operational. Street performers entertain near the fountain. Pickpockets most active during this time. Guards increase patrols. Best time for trading, information gathering, or meeting NPCs, but also most crowded and chaotic.
- **Consequence:** Increased commerce opportunities. Higher chance of random encounters. Perception checks to spot suspicious activity have disadvantage due to crowds.

## Conditional Events

### Pickpocket Apprehension
- **Trigger:** Player or NPCs succeed on DC 16 Perception check during busy hours
- **Type:** Law Enforcement Event
- **Effect:** A pickpocket is caught in the act by Captain Torrhen or one of his guards. The thief is apprehended publicly near the fountain. Captain Torrhen questions them, recovers stolen goods, and hauls them to the guard tower for justice. Crowd gathers to watch. Serves as warning to other potential criminals.
- **Consequence:** Demonstrates the guard's effectiveness. Stolen goods returned to victims. Crime in the square decreases temporarily. Captain Torrhen's reputation reinforced.

### Traveling Performer Troupe
- **Trigger:** Random chance (20%) when player enters Market Square, or player asks about entertainment
- **Type:** Social/Entertainment Event
- **Effect:** A troupe of traveling performers (musicians, acrobats, or storytellers) sets up near the fountain and performs for coins. Crowd gathers, creating a festive atmosphere. Performers share tales from distant lands and may offer side quests or rumors. Entertainment lasts 1-2 hours before the troupe moves on.
- **Consequence:** Opportunity for information, entertainment, and quest hooks. Crowd distraction may benefit or hinder player activities.

## Recurring Events
- **Guard Patrol Rotation:** Every 2 hours, guard shifts change. New patrol departs from the guard tower and walks the square perimeter. Atmosphere: routine security, visible law enforcement presence.
- **Market Opening:** Every morning at 07:00 (7 AM), merchants open their stalls and shops. Early birds get best selection. Town crier announces any official news from the Town Hall. Atmosphere: fresh start, optimism, commerce beginning.
- **Market Closing:** Every evening at 19:00 (7 PM), merchants begin packing up stalls. Shops close their doors. Crowds thin out rapidly. By 20:00 (8 PM), only a few stragglers and guards remain. Atmosphere: day ending, square transitioning to nighttime quiet.
